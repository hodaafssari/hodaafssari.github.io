Meme Mashup

Hoda Afssari

meme mashup

I used to get this question a lot in El school (the background), a lot of hijabis do (people who wear a headscarf). It’s just one of the “funny”/frustrating questions hijabis get. The distortion around it is meant to show it to be a memory. The 3 faces is how I feel, and many hijabis feel when asked that question (mainly as a joke). Thank God for My Reefer shows how one feels when they know the question is going to be asked, the second is when its asked. Wojak shows how others see your face but what actually is going underneath the mask. The hijab is also like a mask; it covers up more.
