This is the first project I did using coding. I made an avatar on P5JS. My avatar is based off a figure I used to doodle in Elementary and Middle School when I was bored. It was for some reason entertaining for me and calmed me to make a character out of simple shapes like rectangles, squares and circles. Lately I have been very nostalgic and wanting to go back to that time where things were more simple and easier as I have felt very overwhelmed lately. The black square around its face is suppossed to represent the hijab, a headscard muslim girls wear. It also represents how society kind of tries to put muslims into a box, trying to understand them better when they are more dynamic than that. The mouth size changes with mic input, and the circle on the bottom moves kind of the opposite way of the mouse to represent how lately I have felt like things dont go quite my way and I dont have much control. The smaller circle randomly chooses a point and grey color in different shades. It represents the same and how my brain feels like it has been glitching. The colors have been my favorite colors for as long as I can remember.
